Our Director

The Taj Security Agency was founded by Col (Retd) C P Singh, a veteran of the Indian Army with an illustrious track record spanning 30 years implementing physical security of premises of national importance.

Post his army career, he modernized the security of the Vadgaon campus (15000 students across 20 colleges) and trained 350+ security guards/ supervisors of Sinhgad Technical Education Society as its Director.
